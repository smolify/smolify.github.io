# Enterprise Account Executive

Our team members work remotely across the globe with the flexibility to work from anywhere and at any time. Our people experience this as a massive benefit! Operating with a strong sense of ownership and collaboration, our teams prioritize results while empowering each individual to do their best work.

---

### About this role

*   **Full Sales Lifecycle:** Manage the end-to-end sales process - from initial outreach and lead qualification to complex contract negotiations and closing.
*   **Territory Strategy:** Develop and execute a strategic plan to penetrate key industries (Finance, E-commerce, Healthcare, etc.) within the region.
*   **Solution Selling:** Conduct deep discovery to understand prospect pain points and demonstrate how Smolify’s dataset and AI features provide a competitive edge.
*   **Community Engagement:** Work closely with our Developer Relations and Marketing teams to leverage our massive open-source community as a source of enterprise opportunities.
*   **Pipeline Management:** Maintain accurate forecasts and CRM records (Salesforce/HubSpot) to ensure predictable revenue growth.

---

### What we are looking for

*   **Experience:** 2+ years of B2B SaaS sales experience, preferably in infrastructure, databases, or AI/ML.
*   **US (West) Expertise:** Proven track record of hitting and exceeding quotas within the US West Coast market, including familiarity with regional procurement and legal nuances.
*   **Technical Fluency:** You do not need to be a developer, but you should be able to comfortably discuss APIs, LLMs, vector embeddings, and cloud architecture (AWS/GCP/Azure).
*   **Communication:** Exceptional verbal and written communication skills in English.
*   **Mindset:** A 'builder' mentality. You thrive in the ambiguity of a fast-growing startup and enjoy creating your own playbooks.
*   Training in sales methodologies (e.g., MEDDIC, SPIN, Challenger) is highly desirable.
*   Quick learner with high emotional intelligence (EQ) and the ability to influence key stakeholders.
*   Passionate about advancing your career in enterprise sales and deepening your knowledge of our products.
*   Aligned with our company values and culture.
